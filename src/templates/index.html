Domain Specific Program Synthesis

This tool uses Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning techniques to solve a propositional logic word problem. It takes facts and questions as input, and determines the results.

For eg. , Facts: If it rains today, I will take an umbrella.
I did not take an umbrella today.
Question: Did it rain today?
Result: Did it rain? False.

The tool is also capable of handling 'Wh' questions: Who, Where, and What

E.g. Facts: If John and Susan go to school, Jack will go home. Susan went to school and Jack did not go home.
Question: Did John go to school? Who went to school?
Result: Did John go to school? False
Who went to school? Susan
